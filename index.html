Predictions Points Breakdown

Understand how points are scored for your group and knockout predictions.

Group Stages

  • Correct team to advance to Knockouts: 5 Points each.
  • Maximum possible points: 160 Points (32 advancing teams).
  • Advancing teams include the top 2 from each of Groups A–L, plus the 8 best third-place finishers.

Knockout Matches

  • Round of 32 Winner (reaches Round of 16): 10 Points (Max: 160 pts).
  • Round of 16 Winner (reaches Quarters): 20 Points (Max: 160 pts).
  • Quarters Winner (reaches Semis): 40 Points (Max: 160 pts).
  • Semis Winner (reaches Gold Final): 80 Points (Max: 160 pts).
  • 3rd Place Match Winner (reaches Bronze): 40 Points (Max: 40 pts).
  • Tournament Champion (reaches Gold): 160 Points (Max: 160 pts).

The "Any-Path" Progressive Rule

Points in the knockout rounds are awarded for a correct predicted team regardless of whether they got to that stage via your predicted path. If your predicted team reaches a round in the official tournament, you get the progression points for that round!
